Problem Report

In order for you to be able to report your problem to our support team, use the alternative boxes within the page as your problem may already have a Self-Fix Method to it. If the problem appears to not be on the page, navigate to the Contact Us section and choose whichever options suits best. Alternate option is to answer the form linked here.

Self-Fix Methods

    Q: My phone screen is cracked, what can/should I do?
    A: Majority of phone places will be able to fix phone screens however you can follow the guide: here to fix the screen yourself.
    Q: I am experiencing problems with connectivity issues.
    A: A simple phone restart can aid in resolving the connectivity issues as this resets the phone's network settings. If this does not work, try TroubleShooting your WiFi.
    Q: My apps are running slow
    A: Make sure your apps and software are updated to the latest releases and also clear Cache and Data on the app or system you are attempting to use. If this does not seem to fix the problem, deleting and re-installing the troublesome app aswell as a factory reset could be a possible fix to the problem you are experiencing.
    Q: How do I resolve battery issues with my mobile device?
    A: In order to address battery problems on your mobile device, check for any background apps that may be draining the battery and force stop or close them. If the issue persists, you could try replacing the battery with a new one or deep-cleaning your phone due to build up of debris.
